🚀 What is ASD ICON?

ASD ICON is a premium business networking and trading platform. Connect with entrepreneurs, trade in vote-based markets, earn tournament rewards, and transact with Litecoin (LTC) securely.

📌 Features:

  • 📋 Business Posts – Advertise your services globally
  • 📊 Vote Trading – Buy/Sell on real-time polls

  • 💳 LTC Payments – Deposit & Withdraw with Litecoin
